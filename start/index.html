Why this exists

It’s easy to forget what you actually did

Between appointments, sessions blur together. A simple record beats guessing.

Progress is hard to notice week to week

Small improvements don’t feel obvious day-to-day, so motivation drops.

Most tools are built for fitness, not rehab

Workout apps can be noisy and overbuilt. Notes and spreadsheets don’t stick.

Physio Tracker is a lightweight digital version of a physio notebook — nothing more.

What it helps you do

Stay consistent

See what you’ve already done without overthinking it.

Notice patterns

Track how things feel over time, not just what you completed.

Prepare for appointments

Bring a clear record to your physio instead of relying on memory.
