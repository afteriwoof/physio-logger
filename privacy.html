Privacy

Physio Tracker is local-first. Your entries are stored on your device using browser storage.

No accounts. We don't run logins and we don't store your exercise data on a server.

Export/import is optional. If you export your data, you control where the file goes and who you share it with.

Minimal web analytics. We use Google Analytics to understand general app usage (for example, which features are used). We don't collect personal health data, store exercise data remotely, or use analytics for advertising.

Feedback submissions. You can send feedback anonymously using the feedback form or by email. Feedback submissions are handled by a third-party service (Formspree) and may be processed on their servers.
