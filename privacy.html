Privacy

Physio Logger is local-first. Your entries are stored on your device using browser storage.

No accounts. We don’t run logins and we don’t store your exercise data on a server.

No analytics backend. This site does not include a tracking/analytics service that sends your usage data to us.

Export/import is optional. If you export your data, you control where the file goes. If you share it, that’s your choice.

Feedback email. If you email feedback, your email provider and ours will process that message as normal email.

Questions? Email aiinnovationdesignlab@gmail.com.
